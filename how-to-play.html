HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to SPACE GLASS! Your goal is to reach the highest score possible by completing the challenges in the game.

You can earn points by completing each level successfully. The more points you score, the higher you climb on the leaderboard!

Before you start, make sure you have enough lives. Each life costs R0.50, and you can buy lives in your Wallet.

Top 3 players at the end of each game session win prizes. Check the Prizes tab for details.

Good luck and have fun!
